Description

When the world ends people will still need hamburgers.

Disaster Diner is an action-packed cooking simulator that takes multitasking to a whole new level. It accomplishes the goal of teaching players something useful without feeling like an educational game.

Play as Barney Burgundy, a man who lives and breathes multitasking. The game starts simple but as every level introduces a new minigame or mechanic you must play on top of the others you'll soon find yourself multitasking like crazy. At its peak you will be, taking orders from customers and ordering food that will be drop delivered by plane, catching the food midair and then running back and forth managing 5 cooking machines, all the while dodging zombie attacks and fighting them off with a shotgun, and taking aim and shooting down bombs dropping down on your building. Anyone can do all this, one task at a time, but if you are up for a challenge the game will keep up with you and reward you for multitasking.

Features

  • Disaster Diner is a hilarious multitasking extravaganza for PC and mobile; you try to serve customers as fast as possible while defending against zombies, raccoons, bombs, pirates, and more.
  • Full of bizarre levels, each introducing a new mechanic or twist to the player
  • The ultimate multitasking game will require players to master multiple thumb-twisting skills as it builds up to the intense endless infinity mode, where you can pit yourself against other players on the leaderboards.
  • procedural generation, dynamic difficulty systems, and a dynamic soundtrack make the game feel different every time you play.
  • A witty, wacky story with loveable characters
  • Expertly-crafted game mechanics that let you manage and defend your entire restaurant at once!
  • Featuring a dynamic soundtrack and 3d sound that responds to your actions, and hand-crafted sound effects! (Yes, we recorded actual food!)
  • 4 Endless arcade modes to test your skills on the leaderboards

About Game Sauté

Boilerplate
At Game Sauté we are dedicated to making wholesome and ethically designed original games. Games can have a huge impact on the way players think. All our games are designed around a core positive benefit from playing them. Blazing Tales Inspires you to be more creative. Disaster Diner teaches you how to manage stress better, Picking Flowers asserts on mindfulness. We decide on a wholesome core and design everything else in the game around it.
