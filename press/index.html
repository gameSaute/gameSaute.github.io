Description

Game Sauté is dedicated to making wholesome and ethically designed original games. Games can have a huge impact on the way players think. All our games are designed around a core positive benefit from playing them. Blazing Tales Inspires you to be more creative. Disaster Diner teaches you how to manage stress better, Picking Flowers asserts on mindfulness. We decide on a wholesome core and design everything else in the game around it.

History

Sage

Sage lives and helps out on their family's fruit orchard carved into the middle of a National Forest. Sage gets enough income to pay for rent, food and not much else.An unreliable satellite internet, lack of cell service and being hours away from any major city keep Sage isolated. They have a tiny house where they live alone with their cat. They spend their time making games, hiking in nature and writing. "I grew up never having the money or transportation to afford to go places. But I had a computer, and with time I taught myself to make fun places for me to explore. Life feels less lonely when I am making it myself digitally."

Basil

Basil lives in San Francisco in an old Victorian house kept in their family for generations. Basil first got to experience having their art in a video game back in June 2017, while during summer vacation they made art for Picking Flowers. Having completed their Civic Engineering Degree and then losing their job due to covid, Basil finally has time to draw as much as they always wanted to. Basil loves Sages games and is thrilled to be taking part in making more games while working to publish and distribute some of their favorites of Sage's creations, previously only distributed among a closed group of friends.
